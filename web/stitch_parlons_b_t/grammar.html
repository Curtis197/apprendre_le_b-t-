The Power of Tones (Suu)

Tonal Phonology

Bété is a tonal language. The pitch at which a word is spoken completely changes its meaning. There are four primary registers.

Gbā High Tone

Translation: To tell / Dire

Ascending pitch, similar to a question in English.

Gbà Low Tone

Translation: Leopard / Léopard

Deep resonance from the chest.

Verb Conjugation: The Aspect Model

Bété focuses on the **Aspect** (whether an action is complete or ongoing) rather than just the time it occurred.

Perfective (Finished)

N’ lī nā

I have eaten / J'ai mangé

Imperfective (Ongoing)

N’ dhī lī

I am eating / Je suis en train de manger
